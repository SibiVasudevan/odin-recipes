Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer makhani (also called paneer butter masala) is a slightly sweet creamy dish of paneer, originating from the Indian subcontinent, in which the gravy is prepared usually with butter (makhan), tomatoes, cashews or cream.[7] Spices such as red chili powder and garam masala are also used to prepare this gravy.

A survey found that paneer butter masala was one of the top five foods ordered in India.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Cloves
  • Cashew
  • Chilli
  • Onion
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Paneer
  • Cream

Steps

  1. In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp butter
  2. add 5 cloves, 2 pods cardamom and saute on low flame until it turns aromatic.
  3. further add 3 tomato, 15 cashew and saute for 2 minutes.
  4. pass the curry to the filter to get rid of seeds and skin.
  5. smooth and silky, the tomato-onion paste is ready. keep aside.
  6. now add 1 onion and saute until onions turn and golden brown.
  7. saute until the spices turn aromatic. make sure not to burn the spices.
  8. add in prepared onion tomato paste and mix well.
  9. finally, enjoy paneer butter masala with roti or naan.
